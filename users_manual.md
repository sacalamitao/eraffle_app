# User Manual

_This manual is a living guide. It will be updated continuously as new features are added to the app._

## 1) User Identity and Roles (How it works for everyone)

In this app, you only need **one account**.

You are not locked into a fixed role during sign-up.

Instead, your role is based on what you are doing at that moment:

- If you **create a raffle**, you are the **Host** for that raffle.
- If you **join a raffle**, you are a **Participant** in that raffle.

This means you can be both:

- A participant in someone else's raffle
- A host of your own raffle

All using the same account.

---

## 2) What you will see on your dashboard

Your dashboard will adapt based on your activity:

- If you have not created any raffle yet, you will see a clear **Create Your First Raffle** action.
- If you joined raffles, you will see a **My Joined Raffles** section.
- If you created raffles, you will see a **My Hosted Raffles** section.

---

## 3) Account access (Sign-up and Login)

All users follow the same sign-up and login process.

There is no separate registration flow for hosts or participants.

---

## 4) Fairness and trust rule

To protect fairness:

- You **cannot join your own raffle** as a participant.

This keeps draws fair and prevents self-entry in raffles you host.

---

## 5) Summary

This app follows a **flexible one-user model**:

- One account
- Multiple possible actions
- Role depends on context (hosting or joining)

This makes the experience simpler, faster, and more natural for all users.
